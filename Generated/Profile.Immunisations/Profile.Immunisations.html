Experimental

    The following structure definitions are available in this profile.

    NameTypeDescription & Constraints
    DCH-Bundle-1BundleThis Bundle resource is constrained to represent a set of resources for a Child Health event.
    DCH-MessageHeader-1MessageHeaderThis MessageHeader resource is constrained to represent a message header for a Child Health Event.
    CareConnect-Organization-1OrganizationThe Organization resource represents the organisation that employs the healthcare professional.
    CareConnect-DCH-Patient-1PatientThe Patient resource represents the patient involved in the provision of healthcare related services, with further constraints to represent a Patient as a subject of a Child Health event.
    DCH-QuestionnaireResponse-1QuestionnaireResponseThis Questionnaire Response resource is constrained to represent a questionnaire response for a Child Health event.
    CareConnect-DCH-Encounter-1EncounterThe encounter resource represents an encounter between a care professional and the patient (or patient's record), with further constraints applied to represent a Child Health Event.
    DCH-Appointment-1AppointmentThis Appointment resource is constrained to represent the appointment for a Child Health event, for example to fulfill a ProcedureRequest.
    DCH-ProcedureRequest-1ProcedureRequestThis ProcedureRequest resource is constrained to represent a request for a procedure to be performed during a Child Health event.
    CareConnect-DCH-Procedure-1ProcedureThis Procedure resource records an action that is or was performed on a patient, with further constraints applied for a procedure performed during a Child Health event.
    CareConnect-DCH-BCGVaccinationRequirement-Condition-1ConditionThis Condition resource records detailed information about conditions or diagnoses recognised by a clinician, with further constraints applied to represent a BCG vaccination requirement finding.
    CareConnect-DCH-VaccinationRequirement-Condition-1ConditionThis Condition resource records detailed information about conditions or diagnoses recognised by a clinician, with further constraints applied to represent a vaccination requirement finding.
    CareConnect-DCH-Immunization-1ImmunizationThis Immunization Resource describes the event of a patient being administered a vaccination or a record of a vaccination as reported by a patient, a clinician or another party and may include vaccine reaction information and what vaccination protocol was followed. Further constraints have been applied to represent an Immunisation performed during a Child Health event.
    CareConnect-DCH-BreastfeedingStatus-Observation-1ObservationThe Observation resource is used for tracking the current and historical observations that have been made for a patient, with further constraints applied to represent a Breastfeeding Status observation for a Breastfeeding Status Event.
    CareConnect-DCH-FirstMilkFeed-Observation-1ObservationThe Observation resource is used for tracking the current and historical observations that have been made for a patient, with further constraints applied to represent a First Milk Feed observation for a Breastfeeding Status Event.
    CareConnect-Practitioner-1PractitionerThe Practitioner resource represents the healthcare professional directly or indirectly involved in the provision of healthcare related services.
    CareConnect-Location-1LocationThe Location resource provides information and details on the physical location and the services provided.
