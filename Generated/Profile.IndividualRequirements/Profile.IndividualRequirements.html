Alpha

    The following structure definitions are available in this profile.

    NameTypeDescription & Constraints
    DCH-Bundle-1BundleThis Bundle resource is constrained to represent a set of resources for a Child Health event.
    DCH-MessageHeader-1MessageHeaderThis MessageHeader resource is constrained to represent a message header for a Child Health Event.
    CareConnect-DCH-Organization-1OrganizationThe Organization resource represents a formally or informally recognized grouping of people or organizations formed for the purpose of achieving some form of collective action, including a Child Health Event.
    CareConnect-DCH-Team-Organization-1OrganizationThe Organization resource represents a formally or informally recognized grouping of people or organizations formed for the purpose of achieving some form of collective action. Further constraints have been applied to represent a team managing a HealthcareService within the scope of a Child Health event.
    CareConnect-DCH-Patient-1PatientThe Patient resource represents the patient involved in the provision of healthcare related services, with further constraints applied to represent a patient as the subject of a Child Health Event.
    CareConnect-DCH-Encounter-1EncounterThe encounter resource represents an encounter between a care professional and the patient (or patient's record), with further constraints applied to represent an encounter for a Child Health Event.
    CareConnect-DCH-CognitionFinding-Condition-1ConditionThis Condition resource records detailed information about conditions or diagnoses recognised by a clinician, with further constraints applied to represent an indicator of cognitive impairment to be considered when communicating.
    CareConnect-DCH-CommunicationProfessional-Condition-1ConditionThis Condition resource records detailed information about conditions or diagnoses recognised by a clinician, with further constraints applied to represent a requirement that a communication professional be present in order to provide accessibility.
    CareConnect-DCH-CommunicationSupport-Condition-1ConditionThis Condition resource records detailed information about conditions or diagnoses recognised by a clinician, with further constraints applied to represent the capability and support required in order to provide accessibility.
    CareConnect-DCH-Condition-1ConditionThis Condition resource records detailed information about conditions or diagnoses recognised by a clinician, with further constraints applied within the scope of a Child Health event.
    CareConnect-DCH-ContactMethod-Condition-1ConditionThis Condition resource records detailed information about conditions or diagnoses recognised by a clinician, with further constraints applied to represent the requirement for a specific contact method in order to provide accessibility.
    CareConnect-DCH-EducationalAssessmentFinding-Condition-1ConditionThis Condition resource records detailed information about conditions or diagnoses recognised by a clinician, with further constraints applied to represent the outcome of an educational assessment.
    CareConnect-DCH-InformationFormat-Condition-1ConditionThis Condition resource records detailed information about conditions or diagnoses recognised by a clinician, with further constraints applied to represent requirements for information in a specific format in order to provide accessibility.
    CareConnect-DCH-MobilityFinding-Condition-1ConditionThis Condition resource records detailed information about conditions or diagnoses recognised by a clinician, with further constraints applied to represent findings for a child's personal physical movement between two spaces that achieves participation and a degree of independence.
    CareConnect-DCH-SpecialEducationalNeed-Condition-1ConditionThis Condition resource records detailed information about conditions or diagnoses recognised by a clinician, with further constraints applied to represent the type of special educational need that the child has.
    CareConnect-DCH-Medication-1MedicationThis Medication Resource is primarily used for the identification and definition of a medication prescribed within the scope of a Child Health event.
    CareConnect-DCH-GPOptOutIndicator-Observation-1ObservationThe Observation resource is used for tracking the current and historical observations that have been made for a patient, with further constraints applied to represent whether the parent or carer decided not to register with a GP.
    CareConnect-DCH-IndividualNeedsPerson-Observation-1ObservationThe Observation resource is used for tracking the current and historical observations that have been made for a patient, with further constraints applied to represent an indicator of whether the individual requirements relate to the child or their primary carer.
    CareConnect-DCH-Observation-1ObservationThe Observation resource is used for tracking the current and historical observations that have been made for a patient, with further contraints applied within the scope of a Child Health event.
    CareConnect-DCH-Procedure-1ProcedureAn action that is or was performed on a patient, within the scope of a Child Health event.
    DCH-EpisodeOfCare-1EpisodeOfCareThis EpisodeOfCare resource has been constrained to represent a Professional Contact Association between a Care Professional or a Healthcare Service with a subject for a Child Health event.
    DCH-RelatedPerson-1RelatedPersonThis RelatedPerson resource has been constrained to represent a person related to the patient subject to the Child Health Event.
    CareConnect-DCH-Practitioner-1PractitionerThe Practitioner resource represents the healthcare professional directly or indirectly involved in the provision of healthcare related services as part of a Child Health Event.
    DCH-HealthcareService-1HealthcareServiceThis HealthcareService resource is constrained to represent a Child Health event service.
    CareConnect-DCH-DischargeDestination-Location-1LocationThe Location resource provides information and details on the physical location and the services provided, with further constraints to represent the discharge destination for a Child Health event.
    CareConnect-DCH-Location-1LocationThe Location resource provides information and details on the physical location and the services provided during a Child Health Event.
    CareConnect-DCH-SourceOfAdmission-Location-1LocationThe Location resource provides information and details on the physical location and the services provided, with further constraints to represent the source of admission for a Child Health event.
